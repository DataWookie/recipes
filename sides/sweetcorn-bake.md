# Sweetcorn Bake

## Ingredients

- 2 tins sweetcoen (creamed or whole)
- 4 eggs
- 60 ml butter
- 60 ml flour
- 60 ml sugar
- 15 ml baking powder

## Method

1. Mix the sweetcorn, eggs and butter in a large bowl.
2. Add the flour, sugar and baking powder. Mix.
3. Bake uncovered at 180 Â°C for about an hour.
